What You Need to Know

每天 5 次够用吗？ Is 5 uses daily enough?
目前每天 5 次完全免费，足以应对日常求职/创作需求。未来如有更多需求会考虑推出付费版。 5 uses are completely free daily — enough for regular job searching and content creation. Paid plans may come if demand grows.
我的内容会被保存吗？ Is my content stored?
不会。你输入的内容只用于当次 AI 处理，不会在服务器端存储。验证邮箱仅用于识别每日额度，不用于任何其他目的。 No. Your content is used only for the current AI request and is never stored server-side. Email verification is only for daily quota management.
生成的内容可以直接用吗？ Can I use the output directly?
可以作为很好的初稿和参考，但建议在使用前通读一遍，根据实际情况调整。AI 是辅助工具，最终质量还是由你把关。 Great as a first draft and reference — but always review before use. AI assists; you're in charge of the final quality.
为什么要验证邮箱？ Why do I need to verify email?
邮箱验证是我们目前唯一的反滥用手段。没有它，任何人都可以无限调用 AI 接口，成本会失控。验证一次可以管当天所有工具，无需重复验证。 Email verification is our only anti-abuse mechanism — without it, AI API costs would spiral. One verification covers all tools for the day.
AI 生成的内容版权归谁？ Who owns the generated content?
你生成的内容归你所有。我们对内容没有所有权，也不会声称任何权利。 You own what you generate. We claim no ownership or rights over your content.
